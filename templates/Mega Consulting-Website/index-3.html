Your Trusted Advisory Partner

MEGA CONSULTING CPA LTD, established in Rwanda in 2020, is a renowned advisory company. Our team of seasoned Certified Public Accountants (CPAs), IT Professionals, and business advisors brings a wealth of experience across diverse sectors. Our specialized services encompass financial reporting, tax advisory, audit, management consulting, and more. We unravel complexities, ensuring compliance and optimizing transactions. With an unwavering commitment to integrity, innovation, and collaboration, we provide superior solutions. Our mission is to empower businesses and individuals, while our vision drives us to become a leading consulting firm in Africa, backed by expertise and innovation.
